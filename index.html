How it works

1 · Capture

Select what you're reading

Drag a region or press a global hotkey to grab text from study PDFs, reading apps, websites, or your own materials.

2 · Understand

Read it, word by word

On-device OCR recognises the characters and splits the line into words, with definitions and pronunciation for each one.

3 · Save

Build a personal lexicon

Keep the words that matter to you, together with the sentence you first met them in. Your lexicon, on your machine.

4 · Review

Make it stick

Spaced-repetition review with FSRS scheduling brings each word back right before you'd forget it.

Optional sentence translation. Add your own DeepL API key in Settings and Glyphly will translate whole sentences on request. It is off until you turn it on, and it costs nothing extra — it uses your key, not ours.

A look inside

Privacy-first by design

  • OCR, word segmentation, and dictionary lookup run on your device.
  • Screenshots and recognised text are never uploaded for OCR or lookup.
  • Saved words and review history stay in a local database — no account, no cloud sync.
  • Sentence translation is the one feature that sends text out, and only when you enable it.
  • After setup, capture, lookup, and review work with no internet connection.
